Third Limb

is a teaching and productivity application for the practice of yoga asana, or poses. It is comprised of a collection of yoga postures, and provides for each a basic overview, a guide on proper form for performing the poses, and an exploration of the corresponding yoga philosophy.

Philosophy

Yoga is not a religion, nor a therapy, nor a school, but rather a unification of the three. Its study begins with a few golden rules, the yamas and niyamas.
  • Yamas
    • Ahimsa
    • Satya
    • Asteya
    • Brahmacharya
    • Aparigraha
  • Niyamas
    • Sauca
    • Santosa
    • Tapas
    • Svadhyaya
    • Isvara pranidhana

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new game?
Press the New Game button.

How do I resume a game already in progress?
Press the Resume button.

How do I access the Menu screen?
Press the Info button in the upper right corner of the screen.

How do I control the use of sound effects?
From the Settings screen, use the Sound Effects switch to toggle on/off sound effects.

How do I configure the pet (cat, dog) the player uses?
From the Settings screen, use the Player Pet control.

How do I select which opponent (player, computer) gets the first move?
From the Settings screen, use the First Move control.

How do I configure the game level-of-difficulty?
From the Settings screen, use the Level of Difficulty control.

How do I view the number of games I have won/lost?
From the Statistics screen, you can view the win/loss/draw statistics for the player. The statistics are compiled for each game level-of-difficulty.

How do I reset game statistics?
From the Statistics screen, press the X button at the bottom of the column corresponding to the statistics you want to reset. This will also reset the corresponding Game Center statistics.
